== Description ==

Meet the Background Block – your gateway to seamless WordPress section customization. This powerful block empowers you to redefine the aesthetics of your site. Effortlessly apply solid color, gradient, or captivating image backgrounds to your sections, and now, transform them with mesmerizing parallax effects.

Take control of your design with the ability to set minimum height and padding, ensuring a visually balanced and stunning layout. Whether you're a seasoned designer or just starting, the Background Block makes tailoring your WordPress site a breeze.

Unleash your creativity, add depth and dynamism to your designs, and create a website that truly stands out. Elevate your WordPress experience with the Background Block today!

### Background Builder Features
- **Background**: Choose from various background types – solid color, gradient, or image – to customize your WordPress sections.
- **Parallax Effects**: Create captivating depth with parallax backgrounds.
- **Height**: Set the minimum height for your section, ensuring perfect layout control.
- **Padding**: Apply padding around your content for a visually balanced design.


### How to use Background Builder
- Begin by installing the plugin.
- Add the **Background** block from the **Widgets** block category within the Gutenberg Editor.
- Customize block settings from the settings sidebar on the right.
- Enjoy!

== Installation ==

### From Gutenberg Editor:
1. Go to the Block/Gutenberg Editor.
2. Search For **Section Background** block.
3. Click on the **Section Background** to add the block.

### Download & Upload:
1. Download the **Section Background Builder** plugin (*.zip file*).
2. In your admin area, go to the **Plugins** menu and click on **Add New**.
3. Click on **Upload Plugin** and choose the **`background-block.zip`** file and click on **Install Now**.
4. Activate the plugin.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I get started with this plugin? =
It's simple! Just install the plugin and add the block to your Gutenberg Block Editor page.

= How to apply parallax effects to my section using the Background Block? =
From the block's right sidebar settings, find the *Scroll Animation* option. Select *parallax* and adjust the speed as desired.

= Is this plugin compatible with my current theme? =
This plugin is compatible with the majority of themes. However, if you encounter any issues, please don't hesitate to get in touch with us. Our support team is here to assist you in resolving any compatibility concerns.

= How can I customize the appearance of the plugin? =
After adding the block, you'll see the appearance of a right sidebar that allows you to customize the selected block.
